About Me

I am Kaviarasu K, a passionate video editor with expertise in Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and modern web-based editing tools. With strong creativity and technical knowledge, I transform raw footage into engaging stories. I also explore client-side editing using JavaScript, HTML5 Video, and WebCodecs.

My Journey

My editing journey began during my diploma, where I discovered how much impact visual storytelling can have. Over time, I worked on short films, product teasers, and creative YouTube content. Alongside editing, I also gained experience in sound design and motion graphics. Currently, I am studying B.Tech CSE, which allows me to merge my love for technology with creative video editing.

Vision

My vision is to combine advanced video editing with interactive web experiences, enabling clients and audiences to enjoy immersive, cinematic storytelling across platforms. I want to keep experimenting with AI/ML, automation, and real-time editing tools to push boundaries in the creative industry.
